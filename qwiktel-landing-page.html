FAQ

The questions every Salesforce admin asks.

If we missed yours, ping us — we'll answer in under 24 hours.

Is my data leaving Salesforce?

No. QwikTel Agent is a managed AppExchange package that runs inside your Salesforce org. Queries, visualizations, and saved query history all live within your existing security boundary. We do not pipe your data to an external warehouse.

What Salesforce edition do I need?

Enterprise edition or higher (Enterprise, Unlimited, Performance, or Developer). QwikTel Agent uses standard Sales Cloud objects and respects your field-level and record-level security model.

Do I need a data team to set this up?

No. The entire admin setup is installing the managed package and assigning the QwikTelAgent_Access permission set. Most customers are live in under 10 minutes.

How long is the free trial?

14 days, with full access to every feature. No credit card required. Cancel anytime — no recovery emails, no guilt trips.

How does QwikTel Agent compare to Einstein / Tableau?

Einstein and Tableau give you dashboards. QwikTel Agent gives you answers. We're built for the moment a sales leader has a question and 30 seconds before the next meeting — not the moment they want to spend a week building a dashboard.

Can I customize the prompts and dashboards?

Yes — on the Growth plan and above. Save your most-asked prompts, build custom dashboards, and share them with your team. Each user keeps their own history; shared dashboards stay in sync.
